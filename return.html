Product Return Policy

Learn how to return products and the conditions to follow

1. Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns on products that meet the following conditions:

  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging.
  • Perishable goods (fresh vegetables & fruits) must be reported within 24 hours of delivery.
  • Non-perishable items can be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • Proof of purchase (receipt, order ID) is required.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Opened or consumed fresh produce.
  • Discounted or clearance items (unless defective).
  • Items damaged due to mishandling after delivery.

3. Return Process

  • Contact our customer support team at info@example.com or call +1 613 123 0258.
  • Provide details of your order, product, and reason for return.
  • Our team will guide you through the return approval and pickup/drop process.

4. Refunds

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to your original payment method.
  • Refunds may be partial if the item does not meet return conditions.

5. Exchanges

If you receive a defective or wrong product, we will replace it free of charge. Please notify us within 24 hours of delivery.
